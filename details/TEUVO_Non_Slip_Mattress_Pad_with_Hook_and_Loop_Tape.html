TEUVO Non Slip Mattress Pad

Featuring a premium industrial-strength adhesive, our non slip mattress pad ensures your mattress stays firmly in place without any risk of movement or sliding. The self-adhesive design makes it easy to apply and reuse, offering a cost-effective and long-lasting solution for your home.

Key Features

  • Powerful Adhesion: Reliable grip for crib mattresses, bed frames, and other smooth surfaces.

Perfect for:

  • Crib mattresses – providing a safe and secure sleep environment for babies.

  • DIY projects – a universal solution for non-slip applications in your home.
  • Smooth surfaces – whether you're securing rugs, cushions, or curtains, it delivers consistent hold.
