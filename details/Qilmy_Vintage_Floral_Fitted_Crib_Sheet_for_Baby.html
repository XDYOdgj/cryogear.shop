Vintage Floral Crib Sheet

A soft, breathable, and stylish vintage floral fitted crib sheet for your baby's comfort and safety. Perfect for standard crib and toddler mattresses. Easy to clean and great for baby showers or birthdays.

Soft and Breathable Fabric

Designed with a skin-friendly blend of 95% polyester and 5% spandex, this crib sheet offers maximum softness and comfort for your baby. Its breathable fabric ensures a cool sleeping environment, making it ideal for sensitive skin and promoting a peaceful night's sleep.

Perfect Fit for All Mattresses

Whether you're setting up a nursery or looking for a cozy play space, this vintage floral crib sheet is designed to fit standard crib and toddler mattresses with a deep pocket of 5-9 inches. Elastic Edge

The elastic band surrounding the entire bottom edge keeps the sheet secure, ensuring it stays in place for a worry-free night. This design also helps to prevent dust and dirt from entering the mattress, making it easier to maintain.

Easy to Clean

This crib sheet is machine washable and fade-resistant, making it a practical and long-lasting addition to your baby's essentials. Its durable yet flexible construction ensures it retains its vibrant floral design even after repeated use and washing.

Versatile Design

With its elegant vintage floral print, this sheet adds a charming touch to your baby's room. It's also an amazing gift for baby showers or birthdays, combining both style and functionality in one beautiful piece of baby bedding.
